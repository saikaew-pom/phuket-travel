# Roadmap: Pillar 2 Beaches and Islands

Use this roadmap when creating Phuket Travel 101 content for the Beaches and Islands cluster. Each article should normally be 2,000-3,500 words, depending on search intent, competition, and how much practical detail the reader needs.

## Pillar Strategy

Pillar 2 is the main Phuket discovery cluster. Most travelers research beaches, islands, day trips, snorkeling, sunsets, and where to stay before they decide on hotels, transport, tours, or itineraries.

The goal is to build a connected content hub that does more than publish static "best beaches" lists. Each article should combine:

- First-hand practical travel advice
- Clear search intent targeting
- Strong internal linking to related Phuket Travel 101 guides
- Original or useful images
- A lightweight interactive feature where it genuinely improves the page
- Clear monetization opportunities without making the article feel thin or sales-led

## Production Rules For Every Article

Before writing:

- Identify the primary keyword, secondary keywords, and search intent.
- Check existing Phuket Travel 101 pages for internal linking opportunities.
- Decide whether the article needs a mini-tool, calculator, map, slider, quiz, checklist, or comparison table.
- Decide the conversion goal before writing.

Required article elements:

- SEO title and meta description
- Canonical URL
- Feature image
- 3-5 supporting body images when useful
- Descriptive image filenames and alt text
- At least 3-5 contextual internal links
- Related guide cards
- Article JSON-LD
- BreadcrumbList JSON-LD
- ItemList JSON-LD for list-style articles
- FAQ section only when it adds genuine value
- Clear "last updated" or "last checked" date

Practical experience signals to include when relevant:

- Best time of day to visit
- Best month or season
- Crowd level
- Swimming safety
- Lifeguard availability
- Shade availability
- Toilet and shower availability
- Parking difficulty
- Scooter access
- Taxi or ride-hailing difficulty
- Estimated transport cost from Patong, Old Phuket Town, airport, Kata, or Bang Tao
- Food and drink options nearby
- Who the place is best for
- Who should avoid it
- What to bring

## Internal Linking Rules

Each new article should act as a bridge to the rest of the project.

Use contextual links inside the article body, not only in related cards. Prioritize links that help the reader make the next decision:

- Beach choice links to beach-specific guides
- Remote beaches link to scooter and ride-hailing guides
- Island trips link to packing, budget, itinerary, and weather guides
- Family beach pages link to packing, airport transfer, and itinerary guides
- Luxury beach pages link to Bang Tao, Surin, beach clubs, and budget tools
- Seasonal beach pages link to monsoon, best time to visit, and packing guides

Avoid generic anchor text like "click here." Use descriptive anchor text that explains what the reader will get.

## Image SEO Rules

For every article:

- Use one strong feature image.
- Use 3-5 supporting images for long articles.
- Use descriptive filenames, for example `kata-beach-family-shade.jpg`.
- Use natural alt text that describes the image.
- Add width and height attributes.
- Use lazy loading for body images.
- Use `fetchpriority="high"` only for the main above-the-fold image.
- Use Cloudflare public URLs in final HTML, for example `https://phukettravel101.com/images/example.jpg`.

## Interactive Feature Rules

Interactive features should be lightweight and useful. Avoid adding heavy JavaScript only for decoration.

Good feature types:

- Quiz
- Calculator
- Comparison table
- Filterable grid
- Timeline
- Countdown
- Tap-to-reveal location section
- Checklist
- Before/after slider
- Seasonal status component

Performance rules:

- Keep JavaScript inline or local unless a real external library is necessary.
- Avoid large frameworks for one small widget.
- Make every feature usable on mobile.
- Provide a static fallback if the feature does not load.
- Do not block the main article content from rendering.

## Monetization Map

Best monetization fits:

- Beach guides: hotels, private drivers, scooter rentals, airport transfers
- Family beaches: family hotels, private transfers, day trips
- Islands: Klook, GetYourGuide, boat tours, private charters
- Snorkeling: island tours, gear, boat trips
- Beach clubs: reservations, hotels, dining guides
- Sunset guides: restaurants, private drivers, photography tours

The article should be useful first. Affiliate links should appear where the reader naturally needs to book or compare something.
